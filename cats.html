Cats I Would Love to Adopt!

Brown Tabby Cat (SIC)

Brown tabby cats (also referred to as standard issue cats (SIC) on reddit) are such sweethearts. There are a few that spend time around my home but one that consistently comes back for food and pets. One of my friends, who is a cat mom, told me that cat is a girl. I wish I could adopt her!

Bicolor Cat (Tuxedo)

My unofficial first cat was a feral tuxedo cat that lived around my home. When I first noticed her, I began leaving food outside and ever since, she continued coming back to my house. Some months later, I named her Beyonce because she gave birth to two kittens around the same time the megastar, Beyonce, birthed her twins.

Bengal Cat

If you've ever seen a bengal cat before, you already know they are absolutely, breath-takingly beautiful! One of the many reasons they are so hard to find and adopt for economically feasible prices. I will never lose hope in adopting one though.
